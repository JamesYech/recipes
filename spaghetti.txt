Ingredients:
    - 1 onion diced, small to large depending on your tastes
    - 2 to 5 cloves garlic chopped or minced (again to taste)
    - 1 Green or Red Pepper chopped (if you want)
    - 8 oz portabella mushrooms sliced or diced (as you wish)
    
    - 1 14oz can diced tomatoes
    - 1 14oz can tomato sauce
    - 1 4oz can of tomato paste (or one packet or two tablespoons)

    - 1 to 2 tablespoons oregano
    - 1 to 3 tablespoons basil
    - 1-1/2 tablespoons sugar
    - salt and pepper to taste
    - 2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions:
    1. heat pan over med heat
    2. add oil to pan and let warm
    3. add onion to pan, sautee for a few minutes
    4. add peppers and mushrooms if using, sautee until all vegies are soft
    5. add garlic and sautee for one minute
    6. add tomotoes, sauce, paste, and spices, mix well, bring to boil
    7. reduce to low and let simmer for 20 minutes to several hours
    8. if simmering for a long time watch for reduction and add more sauce as needed
    9. prepare noodles as you will
